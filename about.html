Currently, I'm in my last semester of the Urban Technology program at Taubman College of Architecture & Urban Planning in Ann Arbor.  I started my academic journey at U-M with psychology, so I gravitate towards projects that underscore the profound impact that digital and physical design can have on communities and people — and their experiences and future. I believe that design is a vehicle for change, and data drives action forward. Digital solutions don’t have to be a threat, or something that will begin to make our cities and spaces feel less human, but rather are an incredible opportunity to make our cities and spaces feel more human, more connected, and more beautiful.  

I'm looking for new opportunities!
