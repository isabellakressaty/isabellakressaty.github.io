Cyclist Awareness for Apple Maps

UT 330, Interaction Design and Urban Experiences, Fall 2023
Figma, Illustrator Exploratory Research, User Testing, Prototyping, Narrative Building

Team: Devin Vowels & Siya Shah

This project explores how we might make cyclists feel safer on the road, utilizing a driver-centric tool to offer bikers and biking infrastructure more visibility.
